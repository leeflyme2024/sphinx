```bash
# volume change
amixer cset name='Left HP Mixer DACL1 Switch' on
amixer cset name='Right HP Mixer DACR1 Switch' on
amixer cset name='HP DAC Playback Volume' 118,118
amixer cset name='PCM Playback Volume' 127,127
```
